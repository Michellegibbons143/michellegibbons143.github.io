Hi, I'm Michelle Gibbons

Data Specialist | Pipelines, Modeling, & Analytics

With over four years of experience in data analytics and building end-to-end data solutions, I specialize in turning raw, messy data into systems and insights that teams can trust. My work spans product, marketing, operations, sales, and finance analytics, helping teams track and act on key metrics such as retention, churn, lifetime value, conversion, and average order value.

I have extensive experience with Snowflake and visualization tools including Tableau, Power BI, and Sigma. My work covers the full data lifecycle, from engineering pipelines and modeling production ready data layers to creating interactive dashboards with advanced DAX. I thrive at the intersection of analytics and engineering, where architecture, optimization, and data storytelling come together to deliver actionable insights.

I bring a balance of technical depth and business intuition, collaborating with cross functional teams to define KPIs, establish consistent metrics, and ensure analytics drives confident decision making. I focus on building data environments that scale, empower users, and turn complex information into insights that are accessible, meaningful, and valuable. Currently pursuing a Master's in Data Science, I am deepening my expertise and expanding the impact of data driven solutions.
